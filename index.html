How it works
Simple
as that.

Find a class on the map, spend a Spark, show up. That is genuinely all there is to it.

1
Explore the map
Drag the interactive map to find studios near you. Filter by hobby, search your area — every dot is a real studio with a real discount waiting.
2
Spend a Spark
One Spark, one class. Your Hobbi Sparks unlock the discount at checkout — you pay the studio the reduced rate directly. No faff, no hidden fees.
3
Show up and have fun
Turn up. Try the thing you've been putting off. Meet people who love it as much as you're about to. The rest follows naturally.
Pricing
Pay with Sparks.

Sparks are Hobbi's booking currency. Buy a plan, get Sparks. Spend them on any class, any time. They never expire.
